SMSU Tweet: Your Campus, Your Voice

SMSU Tweet is the go-to platform where everyone at Southwest Minnesota State University—students, professors, and staff—can come together to share ideas, updates, and experiences. It’s a space that fosters open dialogue, connects our campus community, and celebrates the voices that make SMSU unique. Whether it’s sharing achievements, academic insights, or campus news, SMSU Tweet brings everyone together to engage, inspire, and stay connected.
